    User Experience Researcher¶

    Researching users in a user-centered design process involves various methods. A mixed- and multi-method approach tailored to the (research) project is essential to gain valuable raw data that can by analyzed. So far, I actively used the methods and approaches for data collection and analysis listed below (for details, check out my portfolio of projects). These methods and approaches were applied in projects related to the domains of personal information management, e-business & e-government (designing citizen-centric solutions for data management & interactions G2C/B2C and C2G/C2B), service encounter (re-)design, and institutional repositories for the long term archiving of scientific research output.
